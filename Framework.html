Personal Data Protection Practices and Audit Activities

Objectives

Framework.

The Framework addresses activities related to personal data practices and audit activities. It includes several components that need to be addressed by any company to maintain personal data protection through audit activities. The framework shall guide companies to assure personal data protection by recognizing their existence in the first place and then formulating the required measures including policies, procedures tasks and employee’s competencies
